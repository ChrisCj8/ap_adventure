<p>The Map Item Tab lets you create items for this map config.</p>
<p>This tab won't be of much use unless you made the map yourself and have used the logic_apadventure_map_item_count entity or have made a config script for this config, as there is currently no other way to make these items actually do something.</p>
<p>Try to avoid creating more items than you have locations in your config, as otherwise there might not be enough room to place all items if other configs also have a low added locations to items ratio.</p>
